# Daily Log Entry

Add an entry to `examples/DEVLOG.md` for today's development work.

Include:
- **Date and time spent**
- **What was implemented** (features, fixes, improvements)
- **Technical decisions** and rationale
- **Challenges faced** and how they were solved
- **Kiro CLI usage** (which prompts/commands were helpful)
- **Key learnings** or insights

Keep the entry concise (3-5 paragraphs) and maintain the existing format.
